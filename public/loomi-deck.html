What is Loomi

Paste a link.
Get the insight.

Loomi takes any social media post — TikTok video, Instagram reel, YouTube clip — analyzes it with AI, and stores what matters as structured knowledge you can search and use. Then it lets you chat with everything you've ever saved.

🎬 TikTok 📸 Instagram ▶️ YouTube 🤖 AI Extraction 💬 Knowledge Chat

Not another bookmark tool. Loomi doesn't just save — it understands.

How it works

Four steps. Zero effort.

📎

Paste

Drop any link from TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube.

🧠

Extract

Loomi analyzes the content and distills core insights with AI.

📚

💬

Chat with your library. Get answers from what you saved, not the internet.

⚡
